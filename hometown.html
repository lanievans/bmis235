Favorite Spots

One of my favorite spots in Snohomish is Downtown Snohomish. It's a beautiful spot that everyone loves to visit! There is a collection of small businesses, vintage stores, food spots, and a weekly farmers market during the summer!

Another one of my favorite spots is the Snohomish Valley Golf Center. This scenic spot is when I grew up going to with family and friends! With a mini gold course, golfing range, and food to eat, there's something for everyone!
